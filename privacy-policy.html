Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Reminders app ("we", "our", "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the Reminders app, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: Your name and email address, provided during registration.
  • Task data: Tasks you create, including task text, dates, priority, category, status, and assignments.
  • Authentication data: A securely hashed password for account authentication.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the task management service.
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account.
  • Display your tasks and enable collaboration features (task assignment).
  • Send browser notifications about due and overdue tasks (only if you opt in).

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in a secure Oracle Cloud database. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (TLS). Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and are never stored in plain text.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is only accessible to you and other users you explicitly share tasks with within the app.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete individual tasks at any time through the app. To request complete account deletion, contact us at the email below.

6. Cookies and Tracking

The app uses a JSON Web Token (JWT) stored in your browser's local storage for authentication. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics, or advertising SDKs.

7. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data through the app.
  • Correct your information by editing your tasks.
  • Delete your tasks and request account deletion.

9. Security

We implement appropriate security measures including encrypted connections, hashed passwords, and JWT-based authentication to protect your information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
